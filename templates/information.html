Retinopathy Stages

Understanding the severity levels detected by our AI model.

0

No DR

No signs of diabetic retinopathy. Continue regular regular eye examinations and maintain good blood sugar control to prevent future onset.

1

Mild

Mild non-proliferative diabetic retinopathy. Small areas of balloon-like swelling in the retina's blood vessels (microaneurysms) occur.

2

Moderate

Moderate non-proliferative diabetic retinopathy. Blood vessels that nourish the retina are blocked. Some retinal hemorrhages may be present.

3

Severe

Severe non-proliferative diabetic retinopathy. More blood vessels are blocked, depriving several areas of the retina with their blood supply.

4

Proliferative DR

Proliferative diabetic retinopathy. New, abnormal blood vessels grow in the retina. These fragile vessels can leak blood and lead to severe vision loss. Requires immediate treatment.
